What's Included

Complete Elimination. Every Life Stage.

Bed bugs are one of the most difficult pests to eliminate because surface sprays miss eggs and hidden populations. BRD's treatment protocol targets every life stage — adults, nymphs, and eggs — so the infestation doesn't rebound two weeks later.

  • Full room-by-room inspection to locate all activity
  • Treatment targeting adults, nymphs, and eggs
  • Heat and chemical treatment options available
  • Follow-up inspection included with every treatment
  • Re-treatment guarantee if activity persists

How It Works

What to Expect

1

Inspection

Full room-by-room assessment to locate all affected areas — mattresses, furniture, baseboards, electrical outlets, and other harborage sites.

2

Treatment

Heat or chemical treatment protocol applied based on infestation extent. Both methods target all life stages including eggs that surface sprays miss.

3

Follow-Up

Re-inspection scheduled after treatment. If any activity persists, we return for re-treatment at no additional charge.
