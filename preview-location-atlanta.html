Know Your Enemy

Atlanta's Most Common Pest Problems

Georgia's heat and humidity create ideal conditions for a wide range of pests. Here's what Atlanta homeowners deal with most.

🪳
Most Common

German Cockroach

Atlanta's #1 indoor pest. Thrives in warm, humid kitchens and bathrooms. A rapid breeder — a small problem becomes a large infestation fast.
🐜
Year-Round Threat

Fire Ant

Aggressive and venomous, fire ants colonize lawns across Metro Atlanta. Mounds reappear quickly without targeted colony treatment.
🪵
Silent Destroyer

Subterranean Termite

Georgia is a termite hot zone. Eastern subterranean termites work silently underground and can destroy structural wood over years.
🦟
Seasonal Peak

Mosquito

Atlanta's humid summers keep mosquito season intense and long. Standing water anywhere on your property is a breeding ground.
