What We Treat

Pest Control Services in Maryland

Pest control built for Maryland's humid Mid-Atlantic climate and the species that thrive in it.

General Pest Control

Year-round protection against ants, spiders, roaches, and the many other species active in Maryland homes.

Stink Bug Control

Maryland is one of the hardest-hit states for brown marmorated stink bugs. We treat entry points and interior harborage areas before the fall invasion peaks.

Mosquito Control

Maryland summers are prime mosquito territory. We treat breeding sites and resting areas so you can enjoy your yard from May through October.

Termite Control

Subterranean termites are active throughout Maryland. BRD's treatment plans stop damage before it reaches your home's structural wood.

Rodent Control

House mice and Norway rats are common in Maryland homes, especially as temperatures drop in fall. We find entry points, eliminate the population, and seal them out.

Bed Bug Treatment

Bed bugs are a growing problem in Maryland, particularly in the Baltimore-Washington metro. We provide complete elimination — not just surface treatment.
Know Your Enemy

Maryland's Most Common Pest Problems

Maryland's mix of urban density and wooded suburbs creates ideal conditions for a wide range of pests year-round.

🪲
Fall Invader

Stink Bug

Maryland sees some of the heaviest stink bug activity in the U.S. They pour into homes through gaps and cracks in fall. Once inside, they're hard to dislodge without a plan.
🦟
Summer Surge

Mosquito

The Chesapeake region's humidity and standing water create perfect mosquito breeding conditions. Pressure is highest from May through September.
🐀
Year-Round

Norway Rat

Norway rats thrive in Maryland's urban and suburban environments. They burrow near foundations, enter through small gaps, and can cause significant structural damage.
🪵
Silent Threat

Subterranean Termite

Termites are active across Maryland, feeding silently on structural wood for years before visible damage appears. Annual inspections are strongly recommended.
